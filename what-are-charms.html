What are charms?

Hi!

The charms that you see on this site are made with epoxy resin.
One charm takes about a week to finish completely, some types take a bit longer to do than others. At the moment the two-side shakers take the longest, as the first side has to be completely set for me to be able to start the second side. I use alcohol ink to color the pieces, and various types of decorative elements.
